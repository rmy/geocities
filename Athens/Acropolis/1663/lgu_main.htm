Think room:
Life, God, and the Universe

ONE area that has intrigued me from a very young age is the questions about Life, God, and the Universe.

Does God exist? Did he create the universe? From a practical point of view, how do gods create universes? If the universe is infinite, and God is not inside the universe, where is he? And, not least, what is the meaning of Life?

These are the kinds of questions I explore in this think room.
