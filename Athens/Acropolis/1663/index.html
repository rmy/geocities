Acropolis Academy

WELCOME to Acropolis Academy. Please walk trough the rooms of the establishment at personal whim, and feel free to attend any lecture that may interest you. You will find thoughtful observations, amusing revelations, and original contemplation. You may be especially interested in reading the recorded last speech of Ingemar Lindh -- a truly wise man, and an internationally respected theatre artist and pedagogue. The speech is put here in his memory.

Think rooms:

Life, God, and the Universe

"One area that has intrigued me from a very young age is the questions about Life, God, and the Universe. Does God exist? Did he create the universe? From a practical point of view, how do gods create universes? ..."

The Nature and Meaning of Art

"As a frustrated fiddler in several artistic disciplines I have been forced to spend a lot of time considering the nature and meaning of art. 'Do we create for ourselves, the spectator, or both?', 'What approaches to art are there?', and 'How can artistic expression be useful to society?' ..."


Recreation room:

Our One Award

"The jury said: 'We have visited your web site, and I am happy to announce that your web site, after being anonymously nominated, has been selected for the Phantasm Award.' ..."


I hope your stay here at the academy is a rewarding one. If you are curious about me, your host and guide, here is a Very Short Biography.

Thank you for your visit, and please step in again at convenience.
