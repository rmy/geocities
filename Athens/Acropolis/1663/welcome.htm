AS a kid I dreamt about becoming a Greek Philosopher. My idol was Archimedes, who got great ideas in the bath tub, and then ran naked in the streets shouting "Eureka! Eureka!" ... Even at this young age, though, I knew the dream to be impossible. I could perhaps become a Philosopher, and I could certainly move to Greece, but in my heart I knew it would all be fake.

Then I discovered GeoCities, with its theme cities. I am now very proud to be able to invite you into my Academy here at Acropolis, Athens. It is a true pleasure to have you with me.
